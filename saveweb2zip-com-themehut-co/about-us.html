Welcome to edakkal holidays & relax, Explore, and Enjoy at Our Resort

Nestled in the heart of Wayanad, near the historic Edakkal Caves, Edakkal Holidays is a luxurious retreat that seamlessly blends modern amenities with the natural beauty of Kerala. Our resort is designed to provide an idyllic escape for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and anyone seeking tranquility. Surrounded by lush greenery and breathtaking landscapes, Edakkal Holidays offers a variety of accommodations to suit every guest's needs, from cozy deluxe rooms to spacious family suites and private luxury villas. Each room is elegantly designed and equipped with all modern comforts to ensure a memorable stay. Our resort is more than just a place to stay; it's a gateway to exploring the rich cultural and natural heritage of Wayanad. Guests can embark on scenic trekking and hiking trails, experience the thrill of a wildlife safari, or rejuvenate with yoga and meditation sessions. The nearby Edakkal Caves provide a fascinating glimpse into ancient history, making your stay both relaxing and enriching. With gourmet dining options that showcase the flavors of Kerala and beyond, and a range of activities designed for all ages and interests, Edakkal Holidays promises an unforgettable experience. Our dedicated staff is committed to providing exceptional service, ensuring that your stay is as comfortable and enjoyable as possible.

At Edakkal Holidays, we offer an exciting cycling facility that allows guests to explore the breathtaking landscapes surrounding our resort . Our well-maintained bicycles and curated routes provide the perfect way to experience the natural beauty and cultural richness of Wayanad. Embark on scenic trails that meander through lush forests, rolling hills, and charming villages, offering a unique perspective of the region’s diverse flora and fauna. Whether you are an avid cyclist or a casual rider, our guided tours ensure a safe and enjoyable adventure. Discover hidden gems, breathe in the fresh mountain air, and immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature. Our cycling tours are designed to cater to all fitness levels, making it an ideal activity for families, couples, and adventure enthusiasts alike. Enjoy the freedom of exploring at your own pace while creating unforgettable memories on two wheels at Edakkal Holidays.
